# Initial Concept
The bqopd fanzine platform.

# Product Guide: bqopd

## Product Vision
To demonstrate that fanzines were a form of an internet used by fandom to create community, and to turn that "fanzine internet" back on to allow people to use it again today.

## Target Audience
- **Creators:** Independent artists and writers looking to publish digital fanzines.
- **Readers:** Enthusiasts seeking to discover and consume niche content.
- **Curators:** Editors and power users who manage collections and organize publications.

## Core User Goals
- **Creation:** Easily create and upload digital fanzines.
- **Discovery:** Browse, read, and interact with a diverse library.
- **Curation:** Manage personal profiles and curate collections.
- **Digitization:** Efficiently convert physical fanzines to digital formats.

## Key Features
- **Social Interaction:** Robust features for following creators, liking fanzines, and facilitating community discussions.
- **Curator Workbench:** A dedicated dashboard with streamlined OCR processing tools to digitize physical content.

## Non-Functional Requirements
- **Synchronization:** Seamless synchronization of user data, preferences, and reading progress across all devices.
